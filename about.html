About the Artist

Shari Blue is a Kansas native and has been painting in acrylic, oil and pastel for 15 years. She is most inspired by the Kansas sky and prairie landscape, which are the subjects of many of her works. You may contact Shari at shariblue@kc.rr.com to purchase prints of her work, or if interested in a commission.
